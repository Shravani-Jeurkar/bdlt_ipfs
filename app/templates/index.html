Welcome to IPFS Storage!

IPFS Storage is a decentralized network for file storage and sharing powered by blockchain technology.

Follow the steps below to securely upload and share your files:

  1. User Name: Enter your name in the "User Name" field. This will help identify the uploader of the file.
  2. Upload a File: Click on the "Choose File" button to select the file you want to upload from your device.
  3. Submit: Once you've selected the file, click the "Upload" button to initiate the upload process.
  4. Confirmation: After uploading, your file will be securely stored on the blockchain network, ensuring its integrity and security.
  5. View Uploaded Files: You can view all the files you've uploaded in the section below. Each file is listed with its name, size, and a link to download it.
